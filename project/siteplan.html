Crafting Ideas Website - Site Plan

Site Name

Name: Crafting Joy

This name reflects a joyful and creative experience of making crafts, especially geared toward children and families. It's easy to remember and captures perfectly what the site is about.

Domain: craftingjoy.com

Site Purpose

The website provides a fun and colorful space for kids and parents to find simple craft ideas, complete with step-by-step instructions and printable activities.

Scenarios

  • What are some fun paper crafts I can do with my 5-year-old?
  • Where can I download printable coloring pages or craft templates?

Color Schema

  • Sunshine Yellow (#f9a825) – Used for accents, buttons, and background highlights.
  • Cranberry Red (#b71c1c) – Used for headings and icons.
  • Soft Cream (#fff8f0) – Used for background color.

Typography

  • Comic Sans MS – For headings and titles.
  • Arial – For body text.
